### Introduction

Google&#8217;s supplemental index holds pages which are trusted less. They  
are crawled less frequently, and do not get ranked as highly.

### Getting data

To view supplemental results try this search term [site:www.yoursite.com \*** -sljktf][1].  
Also keep an eye out for how recently pages were cached, this shows  
better than page rank how highly google ranks your content. This tool  
can help &#8211; <http://www.webuildpages.com/cool-cache/>. A good way of checking the strength of your site is to check the supplemental index ratio which is divide

<span style="text-decoration: underline;">site:www.yoursite.com *** -sljktf</span>

site:www.yoursite.com

### Solutions

Ways of getting out of the supplemental index include:

  * Building more links to those pages
  * Put page titles and descriptions as unique
  * Resort your code so unique content is at the top of the page, template stuff further down
  * Increase internal links
